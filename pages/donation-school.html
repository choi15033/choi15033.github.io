JAAGO Foundation

JAAGO Foundation aims for the betterment of the nation through catering the educational needs of children from socially and economically disadvantaged background and empowering the youths along with inspiring volunteerism in Bangladesh.

Somali Community School

The Somali Refugee School was founded in 2011 by a group of 7 Somali students in Malaysia to raise awareness and importance of education in the refugee community in Malaysia. By only having 14 student at first, the school is now proudly educating 70 students from 9 different countries (Somalia, Sudan, Syria, Morocco, Yemen, Tanzania, Ghana, Bangladesh and Malaysia[al-Quran]) from the age of 4 (kindergarten) to 19 (form 2).

Rainbow of Love School

Rainbow of Hope (Pelangi Kasih) is a non-profit school for refugees located in Selayang Lama. As of now, we are one month old and looking for more sustained funding to:
- set up classroom infrastructure

- help to pay rent for the shoplot space

- provide nutritious food for students when we have classes

- buy educational materials

- compensate transport expenses for volunteer teachers and mentors

Fugee Academy

Fugee School ignites the endless passion for learning and growing in every child. They want every Fugee student to realise their full potential and value by equipping them with the right academic, creative and lifeskills in an environment free from discrimination and exclusion. They believe that education is the key that can open doors to a brighter future.

Dignity for Children Foundation
