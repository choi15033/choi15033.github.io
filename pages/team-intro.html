Meet the Team.

Our team is currently based in the school United World College of South East Asia in Singapore. Our passion to help other children who are not as privleged as us was what motivated us to create Orenda. Although our team is only made of 6 people, each person brought a wide of skills from video editing to website development, which allowed us to make Orenda as successful as possible.
