Our Mission.

Orenda was designed to tackle the prolem of quality education, specifically tackling the lack of basic necessities such as chairs, adequate extbooks, whiteboards and related learning materials.

Many schools in Southeast Asia often do not have the basic learning materials necessary to give children a quality education. The schools lack funding, and therefore lack essential resources such as teachers, whiteboards, and textbooks. There are many donors who are willing to finance these schools, especially if they are located in different countries. Our aim is to create a platform to facilitate and act as a resource exchange between schools in need of specific learning materials and organisations, NGOs and individuals who are willing and able to supply aforementioned.
